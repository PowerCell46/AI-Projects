### Choose Your Own Project Topic
WealthBuilder -> The last exercise from the course. FullStack Web Application with authentication, where users can track
their assets, have history of the investment transactions they made, everything all in one place (stocks, cryptocurrencies, precious metals etc.). User-friendly with visuzalizations, aggregations and filters.

### System Architecture – Modules
- Monolith Backend application, no need to overcomplicate it with microservices, multiple databases, message brokers and multiple databases.
- Single PostgreSQL database instance, we aren't planning for big loads and huge amount of multiple users, working at once.
- React Typescript Frontend


### Challenges and Tool comparison
1. What I can think of right now as biggest challenges were the following:
    - Letting the agent instantiate an application by itself and me commiting blindly without checking for a .gitignore. This wasted my time more than a few times,
    making me spend a substrantial amount of time removing all compiled files from the githistory.
    - When creating custom designs and giving the agent prompts for implementing them as views, not mentioning anything about scaling. This made me waste a lot of time, using the tool *https://imvenx.github.io/multiglancer/#* endlessly like this: "On resolution ...x... this button look small, try to scale it up". After learning about skills, I plan to always use the *responsive-design* skill and specifically mentioning in the prompt that I want the view/s to scale well on different resolutions.

2. I implemented a whole frontend application at work, working individually and completed it for about 2 and a half months and the feedback was very good. Back then most of the work I did was with Cusror AI and I was very happy with its performance. The main problem was the pricing - the subscription I fully used for no more than two weeks. Then a friend of mine (also a colleague) told me about antigravity and I decided to use with with multiple accounts (when I hit the free limit I switched to another google account). I was also pretty happy with its performance but I definately enjoyed working with Cursor more. So for answering the question I guess CursorAI and Antigravity have helped me the most.
